Frequently Asked Questions

What's your typical project timeline?

Most projects are completed within 4-8 weeks, depending on complexity. We follow a structured development process: 1 week for planning, 2-3 weeks for design, 2-3 weeks for development, and 1 week for testing and launch preparation.

What's included in your maintenance packages?

Our comprehensive maintenance packages include regular security updates, performance optimization, daily backups, 24/7 monitoring, technical support, and monthly analytics reports. We also offer content updates and SEO optimization services.

How do you handle website security?

We implement enterprise-grade security measures including SSL certificates, regular security audits, malware scanning, firewall protection, and automated backup systems. Our security protocols are updated regularly to protect against emerging threats.

What makes your websites SEO-friendly?

Our websites are built with SEO best practices including optimized code structure, fast loading speeds, mobile responsiveness, schema markup, and content optimization. We also provide ongoing SEO monitoring and optimization services.

Do you provide post-launch support?

Yes, we offer dedicated post-launch support through various maintenance packages. Our support team is available via email, phone, and ticketing system, with guaranteed response times and regular check-ins to ensure your website's optimal performance.
